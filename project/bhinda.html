bhinda nu Shaak



~~>..Bhinda Batata nu Shaak (Aloo Bhindi Subzi – Okra Potato Recipe) is one of common and widely used recipe in Gujarat and North India. It is easy and tasty lunch or dinner subzi. With these simple ingredients, you can make it very quick and easily. This spicy main dish is normally serve with Rotli and any lentil dishes. Must try this classic shallow fry vegetable dish.



bhinda nu Shaak

Rateing
prep time:10 minit
cook time:20 minit
total time:30 minit

Ingredients:

 

                
                250 gm lady’s finger
                2 Potatoes
                

                For Seasoning
                

                2 tbsp Oil
                ¼ of Cumin seed
                Pinch of Asafetida
                ½ tsp Turmeric powder
                2 tsp Ginger chili paste
                2.5 tsp Red chili powder
                1 tsp Cumin coriander powder
                Salt to taste

Instructions:
Wash lady’s finger, potatoes and cut in to long strip. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds in it. When cumin seeds are spluttering, Add asafetida, turmeric powder, ginger chili paste, lady’s finger, potatoes and salt in it. Stir it properly. Cook till both become soft. Also mix cumin coriander powder and red chili powder. Serve with regular dish.
