dal dhokri shak



~~>..Dhokli nu Shaak is one of famous Kathiyawadi Spicy recipes. This spicy curry is quick and easy to made chickpea recipes. There are many other variant of Dhokli nu shaak like Guvar Dhokli nu shaak, Fansi Dhokli nu shaak, Valor Dhokli nu shaak but here I post simple dhokli nu shaak. This spicy chickpea dumpling curry tastes awesome with rotli and kathiyawadi dish.



dhokri nu Shaak

Rateing
prep time:15 minit
cook time:40 minit
total time:55 minit

Ingredients:

 

                
                100 gm Chickpea flour
                Salt to taste
                Pinch of Turmeric powder
                ½ tsp Chili powder
                1 tsp Carom seeds (Ajwin)
                Pinch of Black Pepper powder
                Water according to requirement 

                For Seasoning 

                1 tbsp Oil
                5-6 Curry leaves
                1 tsp Cumin seed
                Pinch of Asafetida
                Salt to taste
                ½ tsp Garam Masala
                1 cup Butter milk

Instructions:
Mix chickpea flour, salt, chili powder, turmeric powder, carom seeds, black pepper powder. Add water and mix well. It should be a batter form. Boil water in a pan or in dhokla maker. Grease dish with oil and pour batter on it. Put dish on pan and cover it. Cook for 10-15 minutes. Remove from stove and cut into small pieces. keep a side. Heat oil in a pan at slow flame. Add curry leaves and cumin seeds, asafetida in it. When it is spluttering, add butter milk in it. Also add salt and garam masala cook for 3-4 minutes at slow flame. Mix dhokli in it. Also cook for 3-4 minutes at slow flame. Serve hot.
