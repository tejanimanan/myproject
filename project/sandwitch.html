Sandwitch Recipe
Rateing
prep time:20 minit
cook time:20 minit
total time:40 minit

Ingredients:

 

                

                9 Bread Slices crusts removed
                100 g Butter
                3 tbsp Mint Chutney recipe here
                1 Cucumber thinly sliced
                2 Carrots sliced into thin rounds
                2 Tomatoes sliced into thin rounds
                6 Green Chillies very finely chopped
                2 Onion sliced thinly
                2 tbsp Coriander Leaves very finely chopped
                Salt as per taste
                Black Pepper Powder as per taste

Instructions:
Butter all the slices of bread. Spread 1 to 2 tsp of mint chutney. Arrange 3 to 4 cucumber and carrot slices on it. Keep one more bread slice on top the cucumber and carrots. Arrange sliced onions and tomatoes. Add chopped green chillies, salt and pepper powder. Sprinkle little coriander leaves on top and cover with one more slice of bread. Press well and cut into squares.
Serve with tomato ketchup.
