Amla Juice (Gooseberry Juice)



~~>....Amla Juice (Indian Gooseberry Juice) is healthy drink. Amla is very healthy fruit and widely used during winter. Amla is enrich with iron, vitamin C and other nutritional benefits. Amla juice is very helpful in digestion, glow skin and hair and hair fall. You can preserve amla juice and can use after season also.



Amla Juice (Gooseberry Juice)
Rateing
prep time:5 minit
cook time:10 minit
total time:15 minit

Ingredients:

 

                1 kg Amla (gooseberry)
                
                Black salt to taste

Instructions:
Grate or cut amla in to small pieces. Grind it like smooth pulp in a mixture jar. Sieve this pulp with cotton cloths. Squeeze cotton with hand properly. Store it for 10-15 day in a refrigerator. As serve time, mix black salt in it.
Amla juice is ready for serve.
