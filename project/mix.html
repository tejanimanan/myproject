Kathiyawadi Mix Spicy Shaak



~~>..Kathiyawadi Mix Spicy Shaak is farmer’s food. Main ingredient of this spicy Kathiyawadi shaak is Eggplant, Peanut and Curd. This is simple but delicious vegetable recipe. In Kathiyawad, Farmer made these kind of shaak at farm with fresh vegetable. I tried this variant on my way. You can add more vegetable in it but eggplant with peanuts gives the best taste. Curd adds tangy taste in this shaak. Serve hot with Bajri Rotla, Green Chili, Garlic Chutney. I am sure, You will not forget taste.



Kathiyawadi Mix Spicy Shaak recipe

Rateing
prep time:30 minit
cook time:20 minit
total time:50 minit

Ingredients:

 

                
                    2 medium sizes Ringan (White Eggplants fine chopped)
                    1 medium size Onion (fine chopped)
                    1.5 tsp Garlic Paste
                    2 cup Groundnut (crushed)
                    1.5 cup Curd
                    
               
                    For Seasoning 

                     1.5 tbsp Oil
                    ½ tsp Turmeric powder
                    2 tsp Red Chili powder
                    1 tsp Cumin coriander powder
                    1 tsp Garam masala
                    Salt to taste

Instructions:
Wash eggplants and cut into very small pieces. Heat oil in a pan at medium flame and add onion and garlic in it. Also add eggplants in it and stir well. Stir till eggplants become very soft. Then add salt, turmeric powder, red chili powder and cumin coriander powder in it. Mix well and add groundnuts and garam masala. Cook for 1-2 minutes. Last add curd and mix well. Stir for few seconds and serve. Serve hot with Rotla .
